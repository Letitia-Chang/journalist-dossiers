# North Star Media & Journalist Dossiers

Internal journalist CRM for **North Star AI Labs** — tracks AI/tech journalists, manages outreach campaigns, and discovers new publications to pitch to.

## What this system does

- **Tracks journalists** with AI-generated relevance scores, contact details, and outreach context
- **Auto-populates profiles** on accept: Claude scores the journalist, fetches their email from the publication website, and finds a profile photo via Google Image search
- **Finds social profiles** via SerpAPI (LinkedIn, MuckRack, Twitter/X) with one click
- **Manages outreach campaigns** with Claude-drafted personalised emails and house style
- **Discovers publications** via RSS feeds, Feedly, Substack, and Medium
- **Logs all outreach** with a full activity feed and relationship history per journalist
- **Tracks press coverage** — articles written about North Star AI Labs

## Access

**App:** https://journalist-dossiers.vercel.app

No installation needed — the app is fully cloud-hosted and shared across the team.
