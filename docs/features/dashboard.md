# Dashboard

The dashboard is your home base — a real-time snapshot of the health of your media outreach programme.

## Pipeline stats

Four cards at the top give you the current state at a glance:

| Card | What it means |
|---|---|
| **Journalists** | Total journalist profiles in the system. Click to open the full list. |
| **Active campaigns** | Campaigns with status Draft or Active (not completed or archived). |
| **Drafts to review** | Email drafts with status Ready or Approved — waiting for your attention. |
| **Sent this week** | Emails marked as sent in the last 7 days. The delta chip (▲ / ▼) compares to the previous 7-day period so you can see if outreach is accelerating or slowing. |

## Campaign pipeline

A list of your 5 most recently updated campaigns. Each row shows:

- Campaign name and type
- **To review** chip — drafts in Ready status (generated but not yet approved or skipped)
- **Approved** chip — drafts approved and ready to send
- **Coverage** chip — press articles linked to this campaign
- Sent count and total journalists in the campaign

Click any campaign to go straight to its detail page.

## Warm contacts

A panel showing journalists in **Responded**, **In Conversation**, or **Covered** status — your warmest relationships. Use this to prioritise follow-ups and identify who to feature first in your next campaign.

## Recent press coverage

The 4 most recent articles added to the Press Coverage log. Each shows title, publication, date, and a sentiment dot (green = positive, yellow = neutral/mixed, red = negative).

## System alerts

Below the main content, alerts fire automatically for four conditions:

| Alert | What it means | Action |
|---|---|---|
| **Overdue follow-ups** | Journalists with status Pitched or Responded whose follow-up date has passed | Log your next step or update their status |
| **Stale journalists** | No new published articles in 60+ days | Check if they've changed beats or left the publication |
| **Needs re-search** | "Find Profiles" hasn't been run in 90+ days | Run Find Profiles to refresh social data |
| **Unreachable publications** | RSS feeds returning errors | Use Sync Feeds or fix the URL in Publications |

No manual monitoring needed — if any of these counts are non-zero, the alert appears automatically.
