# Activity Feed

A chronological log of all outreach across every journalist — your full media relations history in one place.

## What it shows

Every outreach log entry from every journalist, grouped by date (Today, Yesterday, This Week, then by month). Each entry shows:

- Journalist name → links to their profile
- Publication
- Outreach status (Sent, Responded, Covered, etc.)
- Channel (Email, LinkedIn, Twitter, etc.)
- Subject line or message type
- Date

## Filters

- **Publication** — filter to one outlet
- **Status** — filter by outreach status
- **Date range** — from / to date picker

## Summary stats

Top of the page shows total interactions, pitches sent, responses received, and coverage secured across all time (or filtered range).
