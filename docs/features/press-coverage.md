# Press Coverage

Track every article written about North Star AI Labs — mentions, features, interviews, quotes, and op-eds.

## Adding coverage

1. Paste the article URL and click **Auto-fill** — the system fetches the title, publication, and date automatically from the page's metadata
2. Link to the journalist who wrote it (searchable dropdown from your tracked journalists)
3. Set the **type** (Mention, Feature, Interview, Quote, Review, Op-ed) and **sentiment** (Positive, Neutral, Mixed, Negative)
4. Add any notes

## Filtering

Filter coverage by search text, type, or sentiment to see e.g. all positive features, or all mentions in a given publication.

## Why it matters

Linking coverage to journalists builds a record of who has already covered North Star — useful context when deciding who to pitch next and what angle worked.
