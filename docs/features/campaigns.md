# Campaigns

Campaigns let you pitch a group of journalists at once, with Claude generating a personalised draft for each one.

## Campaign types

| Type | Use case |
|---|---|
| Cold Intro | First-time outreach to a journalist |
| Event | Inviting journalists to a North Star event |
| Hackathon | Hackathon announcements and coverage requests |
| Founder Promo | Founder profile / thought leadership pitches |

## Workflow

1. **Create campaign** — give it a name, choose a type, write a brief describing the story angle
2. **Add journalists** — search and select from your tracked journalists
3. **Generate drafts** — Claude writes a personalised email for each journalist based on their beat, recent articles, and your house style
4. **Review drafts** — edit any draft inline before sending
5. **Mark as sent** — once you've emailed someone, mark them sent to update their outreach status
6. **Email Pack** — copy all drafts at once from the Email Pack tab for pasting into your email client

## House Style

Set standing instructions per campaign type under **House Style** (Admin section). These are injected into every Claude prompt — tone of voice, what to avoid, sign-off format, etc.

## Draft generation

Drafts are generated in the background by Claude Opus. The page polls every 4 seconds while generation is in progress. Each draft is personalised using the journalist's beat, publication, recent article topics, and best pitch angle.
